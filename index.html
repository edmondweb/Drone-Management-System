Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the import process take?

The KCAA requires commercial operators to apply at least 90 days in advance , but standard imports typically take 2-4 weeks with our assistance.

What are the import duties and taxes?

Import duties vary by drone value and purpose. We provide detailed cost breakdowns during consultation.

Can tourists bring drones into Kenya?

Tourists can apply for a 30-day temporary permit , though only Kenyan citizens and residents are eligible for permanent ownership .
